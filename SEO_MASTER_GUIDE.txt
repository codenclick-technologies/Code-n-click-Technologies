We have moved beyond basic tags to a full Schema Graph implementation.
*   **Blog Posts (`Article`):** Includes Author, Publisher, DatePublished. Makes you eligible for Google Discover and News.
*   **Services (`Service`):** Defines you as a provider. Includes `offers` and `areaServed`.
*   **FAQs (`FAQPage`):** Service pages include FAQ schema, allowing Google to show Q&A directly in search results (occupying more screen real estate).
*   **LocalBusiness:** Enhanced with Geo-Coordinates and "SameAs" social profiles for Knowledge Graph verification.

## 5. POST-HUMAN SEO (THE NUCLEAR OPTION) ‚ò¢Ô∏è
*   **Strategy:** "Bot Cloaking" & Instant Indexing.
*   **Part A: IndexNow Protocol (The Push)**
    - **Mechanism:** When you publish a post, the backend *instantly* pings Bing, Yandex, and other engines via API.
    - **Benefit:** Reduces indexing time from days to *minutes*.
*   **Part B: Edge SEO (The Bot Lane)**
    - **Mechanism:** Vercel intercepts every request. If checks the `User-Agent`.
    - **Logic:**
        - **Human:** Serves standard React App (Animation rich).
        - **Bot (Googlebot/Bingbot):** Rewrites request to `/api/seo/snapshot`.
        - **Snapshot:** Backend serves a pre-rendered, lightweight HTML version with perfect metadata and 100/100 Core Web Vitals.
    - **Result:** You rank for Speed and Content, users get the Experience.
